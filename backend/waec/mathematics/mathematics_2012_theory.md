**2.** Sonny is twice as old as Wale. Four years ago, he was four times as old as Wale. When will the sum of their ages be 66?

**MARKING SCHEME:**
Explanation Let Wale's present age = x. Then Sonny's age = 2x Four years ago, Wale = x - 4 Sonny = 4(x - 4) = 2x - 4 Sonny = 2x = 2(6) = 12 years In z years' time, the sum of their ages = 66 Wale = 6 + z Sonny = 12 + z Therefore, in 24 years time, the sum of their ages will be 66.
